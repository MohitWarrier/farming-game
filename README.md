# Farming Game

A simple 2D pixel-art farming game built in Godot. You play as a farmer on an open field, using tools to till the soil, plant seeds, water your crops, and harvest them for profit. Progress through days, grow your balance, and keep the farm going.

## Gameplay

- **Till** the ground with a hoe to prepare soil for planting
- **Plant** seeds (tomatoes, corn, etc.) and **water** them daily to help them grow
- **Harvest** fully grown crops and sell them for money
- Advance to the **Next Day** to progress crop growth
- Manage your inventory and money to expand your farm
